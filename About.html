About

Josie Kwan is an undergrad student at Georgia Tech. Her studies focus on media studies, film history, and literature. You can find her at local coffee shops and open mics performing her poetry. You can also read some of her pieces in the student magazine, North Ave Review. Poetry is her favorite way to express herself, but she writes plenty of prose as well.
