Script: Burn the Witch by Josie Kwan

Burn the Witch is the pilot episode of a TV show Josie created for a school project.

Synopsis: As the frenzied mob clamors to burn young Peony at the stake, her sister Sonya battles against the supernatural, family secrets, and the echoes of blame. Burn the Witch invites readers into a world where ordinary lives collide with the extraordinary, exploring themes of resilience, the unknown, and the enduring strength of familial ties.
